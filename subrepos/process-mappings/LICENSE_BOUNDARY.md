# License Boundary

During incubation, this subtree is distributed only as part of
`rac-conformance` under the parent [Apache License 2.0](../../LICENSE). It is not
an independently released package and intentionally does not carry a second
license file that could imply otherwise.

Source manifests must record the rights and reuse conditions of every external
source. A citation or mapping record does not copy, relicense, or expand rights
in the underlying legislation, policy, guidance, dataset, or platform artifact.
Material with absent, incompatible, or unclear reuse rights remains referenced
but is not vendored.

Before an approved extraction, the migration rehearsal must:

1. copy the Apache-2.0 license and any required notices into the proposed root;
2. audit vendored and generated artifacts for compatible rights and provenance;
3. verify attribution and source-manifest links; and
4. record the exact migration commit and rollback procedure.

The future remote becomes canonical only after those checks and the explicit
human cutover gate pass.
