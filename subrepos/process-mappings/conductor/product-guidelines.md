# Product Guidelines

- No runtime AI decisions or agent-certified controlling assertions.
- No mapping without a named consumer and source spine.
- Preserve jurisdiction, authority, effective date, uncertainty, and loss.
- Human legal, clinical, ethical, disclosure, regulatory, and funding decisions
  remain explicit human tasks.
- Golden fixtures require independent human promotion; AI output stays in
  candidate areas with `method: agent-proposed`.
- Use only public/licensed evidence and synthetic health scenarios.
- Do not infer confidential or unavailable facts.
- Keep platform adapters optional and normative contracts external.
