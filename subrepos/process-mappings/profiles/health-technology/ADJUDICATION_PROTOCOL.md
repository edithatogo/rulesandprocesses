# Comparison Adjudication Protocol

1. Separate official process facts, public decision facts, project mapping, and
   unavailable deliberative evidence.
2. Resolve source assertions by authority, jurisdiction, indication, effective
   date, and verification state.
3. Keep `blocked`, `UNVERIFIED`, and `confidential_unavailable` explicit.
4. Treat a regulator decision as independent from a payer/listing decision;
   advice is not a decision and authorisation is not funding.
5. Agents may propose candidates, but a human certifies the source spine and
   comparison limits before any fixture is promoted.

The current packet deliberately selects no case. This is a defensible
no-selection outcome, not evidence that the pathways are equivalent.
