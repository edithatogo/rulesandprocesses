# Adverse-Incident Sources

`SOURCE_MANIFEST.json` is the candidate source ledger for the adverse-incident
and open-disclosure profile. It separates NZ national sources, Australian
national sources, and NSW policy directives; it does not treat a national
framework or accreditation standard as legislation.

Three official documents are currently locator-verified but download-blocked in
this environment. Their entries remain explicit exceptions with no fabricated
digest or substitute secondary source. Mappings remain `agent-proposed` until
the controlling text, effective date, applicability, and authority are
independently reviewed.
