# Expected Results Policy

The kit has two corpus classes:

- Every file under `bundle/pic-semantics-0.1.0/examples/valid/` MUST validate
  against the bundled schema.
- Every file under `bundle/pic-semantics-0.1.0/examples/invalid/` MUST produce
  at least one validation error.

The kit verifies structure only. It does not certify policy meaning, legal
correctness, source authority, or semantic agreement with the maintainer
implementation. Results must include the kit digest and evaluator identity.
