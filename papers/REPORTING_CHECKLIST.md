# Paper reporting checklist

Apply this checklist to each manuscript before any `[HUMAN]` submission gate.

- [ ] Title, abstract, keywords, author metadata, affiliations, ORCID, and declarations are complete.
- [ ] Research question, scope, policy vintage, jurisdictions, and unit of analysis are explicit.
- [ ] Data/fixture provenance, candidate versus approved status, human adjudication, and exclusion rules are explicit.
- [ ] Engine/package/repository revisions, runtime versions, commands, and environment limitations are recorded.
- [ ] Every numerical result can be regenerated from committed inputs and a named script/runner.
- [ ] Every legal or policy claim has a primary-source citation and effective-date boundary.
- [ ] Tables and figures have captions, units, denominators, tolerances, and source/artifact references.
- [ ] Agreement is separated from legal validity; divergence is separated from software defect.
- [ ] Limitations cover selection bias, coverage, oracle independence, adapter assumptions, vintage drift, and generalisability.
- [ ] Funding, conflicts, ethics/data governance, author contributions, and availability statements are present or explicitly not applicable.
- [ ] Journal/arXiv formatting and word/figure limits are checked.
- [ ] Dylan authorizes submission; agents do not submit.
