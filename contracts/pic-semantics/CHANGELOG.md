# CHANGELOG: pic-semantics

## 0.1.0 (2026-07-06)

- Initial release of Policy Interchange Contracts (PIC) value-state and epistemic-status semantics.
- Standardized `valueState` enum: `known`, `not_provided`, `unknown`, `verified_stale`.
- Standardized `epistemicStatus` enum: `observed`, `asserted`, `unknown`.
- Defined normative propagation decision tables for logical and comparison operations.
