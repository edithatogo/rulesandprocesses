# FOI process citation and Zenodo mirroring

Assess and prepare `edithatogo/foi-process` as an in-scope FOI programme
integration companion, while preserving FOI-O as the authority for legal
semantics and profiles.

## Acceptance

- `foi-process` has validated citation metadata and an immutable citable release.
- Its integration boundary is documented in the programme manifest and ledger.
- Zenodo evidence is recorded only after a release is actually deposited and
  its version DOI is verified.
